Innovation at Our College

Smart Irrigation System

This project involves designing an automated irrigation system that uses sensors to monitor soil moisture levels and weather conditions.
The system ensures optimal water usage, reducing waste and improving crop yields. It integrates IoT devices for remote monitoring and control, providing farmers with real-time data and automation capabilities.

Renewable Energy Harvesting

This project focuses on the development of efficient methods to harvest renewable energy from sources such as solar, wind, and hydroelectric power. The team designed a hybrid system that combines solar panels and wind turbines to maximize energy output. The system includes a smart grid for energy distribution and storage, ensuring sustainable and reliable power supply.

Autonomous Delivery Robot

This project involves creating an autonomous robot capable of navigating urban environments to deliver packages. The robot uses advanced sensors, GPS, and machine learning algorithms to detect obstacles and plan efficient routes. It aims to revolutionize the logistics industry by providing a cost-effective, eco-friendly, and reliable solution for last-mile deliveries.
